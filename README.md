# Jazmia Henry

**AI Researcher & Engineer** · Oxford DPhil · Stanford HAI Fellow · NeurIPS Reviewer

I build open-source systems for reinforcement learning, evaluation, and efficient model training. My research focuses on why AI evaluation frameworks fail and how to fix them — from reward system design to biologically-inspired architectures to AI fairness. I care about making powerful AI accessible beyond data centers.

🎓 Taught 1,000+ students through Correlation One · LinkedIn Learning instructor · Two textbook chapters (McGraw Hill, Bloomsbury Academic)

**Currently:** NeurIPS 2026 E&D submission on evaluation validity · Building cognitive architectures for ARC-AGI-3 · Previously at Microsoft, Iso AI (founder), Collide
